Data Analyst

Job Description

We are seeking a motivated Data Analyst to collect, clean, analyze, and
visualize data to support business decisions. The ideal candidate should
be proficient in SQL, Excel, Python, and BI tools, with strong
analytical and problem-solving skills. Freshers with relevant academic
projects are encouraged to apply.

Required Skills

-   SQL
-   Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib)
-   Microsoft Excel (Advanced)
-   Power BI or Tableau
-   Statistics and Data Analysis
-   Data Cleaning and Visualization
-   Problem-Solving
-   Communication Skills
-   Critical Thinking
-   Basic Machine Learning (Preferred)

Qualifications

-   Strong analytical and logical thinking
-   Good communication and presentation skills
-   Ability to work independently and in a team
-   Attention to detail
-   Willingness to learn new technologies

Education

Eligible candidates should possess any one of the following: - B.E. -
B.Tech - B.Sc. (Computer Science, IT, Statistics, Mathematics) - M.Sc. -
MCA - BCA

Eligibility

-   Freshers are welcome to apply.
-   Internship or academic project experience is an advantage.
